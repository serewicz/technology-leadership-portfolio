# Contributing

This is a personal portfolio repository for technology leadership, operating partner, diligence, governance, and board advisory materials.

Suggestions and issues are welcome when they improve clarity, usefulness, or executive readability.

Please do not submit:

- confidential client data
- proprietary company materials
- non-public diligence findings
- private customer, employee, or investor information
- case studies based on confidential work

Case studies should remain synthetic examples or be based only on clearly public information.
