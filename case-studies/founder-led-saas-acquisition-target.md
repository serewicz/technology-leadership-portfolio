# Founder-Led SaaS Acquisition Target

## Scenario

A founder-led vertical SaaS company is being evaluated as an acquisition target. The product has customer traction, but core technology operations depend heavily on the founder and a small number of senior engineers.

## Business Context

The acquirer needs to understand whether the company can be integrated without disrupting customers, slowing product delivery, or losing critical operational knowledge. The diligence question is not only whether the product works today. The question is whether the technology organization can operate predictably after ownership changes.

## Technology Risks

- manual deployments
- founder dependency
- incomplete documentation
- informal security ownership
- unclear production support responsibilities
- limited integration readiness
- weak evidence for repeatable operating controls

## How the Portfolio Applies

- **CTO Operating System** provides the diligence framework, risk scoring model, board questions, and 100-day acquisition integration planning structure.
- **Executive AI Advisor** analyzes diligence documents and generates cited technology due diligence reports, risk heatmaps, and acquisition integration plans.
- **K8s Platform Blueprint** provides remediation patterns for deployment governance, platform controls, observability, compliance evidence, and operational ownership.
- **Engineering Operating Metrics** measures post-close improvement in delivery flow, review quality, rework, risk, and governance.

## Outputs

- Technology Due Diligence Report
- Risk Heatmap
- Board Questions
- Acquisition Integration Plan
- Post-close Metrics Dashboard

## Board-Level Questions

- Which systems, deployments, or vendor relationships depend on the founder?
- What operational knowledge must be transferred before or immediately after close?
- Which manual deployment steps create business continuity risk?
- What documentation gaps could slow integration?
- Which security responsibilities need clear ownership?

## Example 100-Day Priorities

- Document production deployment and rollback procedures.
- Identify successors for founder-owned operational responsibilities.
- Create an integration readiness register with owners and dates.
- Establish production access review and privileged access controls.
- Define post-close engineering operating metrics for flow, quality, risk, and governance.
