# Regulated FinTech Platform

## Scenario

A regulated software company has vendor concentration, privileged access concerns, compliance evidence gaps, incident reporting exposure, CRA readiness questions, and emerging AI governance needs.

## Business Context

The company operates in a compliance-sensitive market where technology risk can quickly become regulatory, customer, and board risk. Leadership needs a clear view of control maturity, evidence gaps, and remediation priorities.

## Technology Risks

- vendor concentration
- privileged access gaps
- compliance evidence gaps
- incident reporting exposure
- CRA readiness questions
- unclear AI governance
- limited audit trail for technical decisions
- inconsistent review quality for high-risk changes

## How the Portfolio Applies

- **CTO Operating System** provides governance templates, board reporting models, risk registers, and AI governance frameworks.
- **Executive AI Advisor** generates CRA readiness assessments, AI governance assessments, security risk summaries, and board discussion points from company documents.
- **K8s Platform Blueprint** provides implementation patterns for policy-as-code, access controls, observability, compliance evidence, and platform governance.
- **Engineering Operating Metrics** measures review quality, delivery risk, rework, cost, and governance exceptions.

## Outputs

- CRA Readiness Assessment
- AI Governance Assessment
- Security Risk Review
- Governance Scorecard
- Board Discussion Points

## Board-Level Questions

- Which compliance obligations require legal or regulatory confirmation?
- Are privileged access controls documented, reviewed, and evidenced?
- Which vendors create material continuity, compliance, or operational risk?
- What evidence exists for incident reporting and response readiness?
- How are AI use cases reviewed, approved, and monitored?

## Example 100-Day Priorities

- Build a compliance evidence register mapped to owners and systems.
- Review privileged access and implement recurring access certification.
- Document incident reporting workflows and escalation responsibilities.
- Define AI governance process for use cases, data sensitivity, and human review.
- Implement policy-as-code patterns for high-risk workloads and evidence generation.
