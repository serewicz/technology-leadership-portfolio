# Growth Equity B2B SaaS

## Scenario

A $5M-$50M B2B SaaS company is scaling but faces cloud cost growth, delivery predictability issues, security governance gaps, AI readiness questions, and technical debt.

## Business Context

The company is preparing for growth equity investment. Leadership needs to show that technology can support scale without margin erosion, delivery volatility, unmanaged AI risk, or avoidable security exposure.

## Technology Risks

- cloud cost growth without clear allocation
- inconsistent delivery predictability
- technical debt in core product areas
- incomplete security governance
- unclear AI readiness and AI usage controls
- limited board-level technology reporting
- uneven platform ownership

## How the Portfolio Applies

- **CTO Operating System** provides the operating cadence, board reporting structure, governance frameworks, and 100-day growth planning model.
- **Executive AI Advisor** generates growth-equity diligence outputs, board technology briefs, AI governance assessments, and 100-day plans from company documents.
- **K8s Platform Blueprint** provides FinOps, observability, platform governance, policy controls, and modernization patterns.
- **Engineering Operating Metrics** tracks delivery flow, review quality, rework, engineering cost, AI usage cost, risk, and governance outcomes.

## Outputs

- Board Technology Brief
- Growth 100-Day Plan
- FinOps Review
- Delivery Dashboard
- AI Governance Assessment

## Board-Level Questions

- Is cloud spend growing faster than customer growth or revenue?
- Which technical debt areas constrain delivery predictability?
- What security governance gaps should be addressed before the next growth phase?
- How is AI usage governed, measured, and reviewed?
- Which platform investments have the strongest business case?

## Example 100-Day Priorities

- Implement cloud cost allocation by product, team, and environment.
- Establish monthly CTO operating review with flow, cost, risk, and governance metrics.
- Define AI usage policy, approved use cases, and data handling expectations.
- Prioritize technical debt based on business impact and delivery drag.
- Create board-ready reporting for technology risk and platform modernization.
